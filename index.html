About the Data

This is the first iteration of a map of all the curb regulations in Chinatown. Line segments represent distances betwen signposts, fire hydrants, parking meters, and curb cuts. These line segments will be used to calculate parking capacity in the future. Click on individual parking signs to see what data the city has stored in Cartegraph! We will be enriching this data in Q2 2025.
